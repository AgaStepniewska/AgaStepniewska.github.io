about me

Hi! My name is Aga, I am a junior Front-End Developer, Artist and a Mum. Since June 2017 I work voluntarily as a Web Designer for Merton Neighbourhood Watch.
I have been interested in the programming about a year ago, but it is my passion since I started the course on April this year. I create responsive, beautiful and easy to use websites, I can convert any PSD design into HTML.

At work I put on, challenges, creativity and development.
